    • Tips on effective participation

    Expect to be challenged (and rewarded!) by the papers we cover. Show up on time and be prepared to participate in discussion. That means that you should read the paper prior to the meeting. All of our time will be devoted to discussion. It is expected that every attending student contributes to the discussion in each meeting.

    It never hurts to write down your thoughts. If you have the time, practice writing reviews of the papers that we read. This will also help you articulate questions/concerns that you have about a paper.

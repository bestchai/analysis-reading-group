Comprehension and Analysis of Large Systems

Analysis reading group

Fall 2014, Fridays 2-3PM, ICCS X


Description

Software analysis is a diverse and fascinating topic that is important to the design and implementation of programming languages, software engineering, formal methods, software verification, systems, and many other areas of computer science.

The objective of the reading group is to read and discuss papers related to software analysis, broadly construed. We plan to select papers based on the interests of those who attend the meetings.

Format

This is a discussion-heavy reading group.

  • The discussion in each class will focus on one paper.
  • The discussion for each paper is...

Tips on effective participation

Expect to be challenged (and rewarded!) by the papers we cover. Show up on time and be prepared to participate in discussion. That means that you should read the paper prior to the meeting. All of our time will be devoted to discussion. It is expected that every attending student contributes to the discussion in each meeting.
